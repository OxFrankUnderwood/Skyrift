Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Skyrift ("we", "our", or "the app") is a weather application developed by Emre Kep. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Skyrift collects the following information solely to provide weather services:

  • Precise Location — When you grant permission, we use your device's GPS coordinates to fetch current weather conditions and forecasts for your location. Your location is used in real time and is never stored on our servers.
  • Manually Saved Locations — City names and coordinates you add are stored locally on your device only (via UserDefaults / App Groups for widget support). They are never uploaded to any server.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display current weather and forecasts for your location or saved cities.
  • To update the Home Screen widget and Live Activity with weather data.
  • To send optional weather alert notifications (only if you enable them).

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering weather information within the app.

Third-Party Services

Skyrift uses the following Apple and third-party services:

  • Apple WeatherKit — Your location coordinates are sent to Apple's WeatherKit API to retrieve weather data. Apple's data handling is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — Used for geocoding (converting city names to coordinates) when searching locations. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source API that does not require authentication and does not store personal data. See Open-Meteo Terms.

Data Storage & Retention

  • All app data (saved locations, preferences, cached weather) is stored locally on your device.
  • We do not operate any servers or databases that store personal information.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

Tracking & Advertising

Skyrift does not track you across apps or websites, does not display advertisements, and does not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Children's Privacy

Skyrift does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not contain any features directed at children.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Revoke location access via Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services.
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling Skyrift.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
